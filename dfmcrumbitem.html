Detailed Description

DFMCrumbItem is the crumb item inside the crumb bar.

DFMCrumbItem is the crumb item which managed by the DFMCrumbBar widget, it holds CrumbData which includes the url of the crumb item, the displaying text, and the icon of the item (if have)

See also DFMCrumbInterface, DFMCrumbManager, and DFMCrumbBar.
